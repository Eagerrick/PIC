Air-Land Drone Charging Station

Smart energy for smarter skies

Who are we?

We are a team of engineering students from Instituto Superior Técnico, brought together by a shared interest in autonomous systems and intelligent infrastructure. Through our work in Projeto Integrador de 1º Ciclo, we aim to transform a problem seen in the drone industry into a practical solution.

What is our project about?

Our project is designed to coordinate the charging of a drone swarm by continuously monitoring their needs, including battery levels and distance to charging stations. The system schedules charging operations intelligently, ensuring efficient operations and minimizing downtime in areas where continuous activity is required

Why choose our system?

  • Continuous drone operation with minimal downtime
  • Optimized energy use for cost efficiency
  • Real-time monitoring and intelligent coordination
  • Scalable solution for campuses, industries, and smart cities
